# Email / Outlook troubleshooting

**Applies to:** staff using Outlook / Microsoft 365 email · **KB ID:** KB-0005
**Last reviewed:** 2026-06-21 · **Owner:** Service Desk

## What this covers
Quick fixes for common email problems: can't send/receive, Outlook keeps asking for your password, a
message bounced, or your mailbox is full.

## The fastest test
Open **webmail** at **https://outlook.office.com** and sign in.
- **Webmail works, Outlook doesn't** → the problem is the Outlook app on your PC (see "Outlook issues").
- **Webmail also fails** → it's your account/sign-in (try [Reset your password](KB-0001-password-reset.md))
  or a service issue — contact the Service Desk.

## Outlook issues (desktop app)
1. **Keeps asking for your password / "trying to connect":** **restart Outlook**, then restart the laptop.
   If it persists after a recent password change, your saved credential is stale — contact the desk
   (reference KB-0005).
2. **Send/receive stuck:** check you're not in **Work Offline** mode (Send/Receive tab) and that the
   internet works (KB-0004).

## "My message bounced" (a bounce-back / NDR)
The bounce message usually says why:
- **"recipient address rejected / user unknown"** → the address is wrong or the person left — check the
  spelling and clear any old auto-complete entry, then resend.
- **"mailbox full / over quota"** → it's the *recipient's* mailbox that's full — not your problem to fix.

## Mailbox full (your own)
Empty **Deleted Items** and **Junk**, archive old large emails (and big attachments). If you still can't
send/receive, contact the desk to review your quota.

## Not getting an email you expected
Check your **Junk/Spam** folder and any **rules** you may have set. If a legitimate sender is blocked,
contact the desk.

## Still not working?
Contact the Service Desk with: does **webmail** work? the exact **error** or **bounce text**, and
**when** it started. Reference **KB-0005**.
