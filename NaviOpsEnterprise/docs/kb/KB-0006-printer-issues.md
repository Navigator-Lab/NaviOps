# Printer issues — quick fixes

**Applies to:** staff printing to office or network printers · **KB ID:** KB-0006
**Last reviewed:** 2026-06-21 · **Owner:** Service Desk

## What this covers
Quick fixes when nothing prints, the printer shows "offline," or your job is stuck.

## Try these in order
1. **Check the printer itself:** is it **powered on**, no **error light**, **paper** loaded, and **toner/
   ink** OK? Clear any **paper jam**.
2. **Right printer selected?** Make sure you're sending to the correct printer (e.g. **`PRT-FLOOR2`**), not
   an old or "Microsoft Print to PDF" default.
3. **Nothing prints / jobs pile up:** open the print queue (Settings → Bluetooth & devices → Printers →
   your printer → **Open queue**) and **Cancel All**, then try one small test page.
4. **"Printer offline":** in the queue's **Printer** menu, make sure **"Use Printer Offline"** is **not**
   ticked. Then try again.
5. **Still stuck:** **restart your computer** (this restarts the print service that handles your jobs) and
   try once more.
6. **Garbled / wrong output:** stop — don't reprint repeatedly; contact the desk (likely a driver issue).

## Still not working?
- Others can print to the same printer but you can't → it's your PC; contact the desk.
- **No one** can print to it → it's the printer; report it with the **printer name/location**.
- Provide: the **printer name**, what you see (**offline / nothing / garbled / error**), and whether it's
  just you. Reference **KB-0006**.
