# Knowledge Base — NaviOpsEnterprise

End-user-facing (and T1-facing) self-service articles. A good KB **deflects tickets** (users
self-serve) and **speeds resolution** (techs follow a known-good fix). Written plainly, owned, and
reviewed so they don't rot (Lesson 28).

- **Published** ✅ = full article in this folder.
- **Queued** ⏳ = planned; written when its lesson lands.
- All examples are sanitized placeholders (`corp.example`).

## Writing standard (Lesson 28)
- Title is the user's goal ("Connect to the VPN"), not the system name.
- Steps are numbered, exact, and include what success looks like.
- Always end with "still not working? → contact the Service Desk with X, reference this KB."
- Every article has an **owner** and a **last-reviewed** date. Review on a cycle; retire stale ones.
- Use `docs/templates/kb-article.md`.
