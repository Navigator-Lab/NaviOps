# Unlock your account

**Applies to:** all staff with a `corp.example` account · **KB ID:** KB-0002
**Last reviewed:** 2026-06-21 · **Owner:** Service Desk

## What this covers
What "your account is locked" means, how to unlock it, and how to stop it happening again.

## Why accounts lock
Your account locks automatically after too many wrong password attempts. This is a **security
feature** — it stops someone guessing your password. A locked account is *not* the same as a
disabled or expired one; it usually unlocks itself after a short wait, or a tech can unlock it now.

> **The #1 hidden cause:** an **old password saved on another device** — most often your **phone's
> email app**, a **mapped network drive**, or **Wi-Fi** — keeps trying the wrong password in the
> background and re-locks you. Fixing the saved password is the real cure.

## Unlock it
1. **Wait ~15 minutes** — many accounts auto-unlock, then you can sign in with your *correct*
   password. (If you've forgotten it, reset first — KB-0001.)
2. Still locked / can't wait → use **self-service unlock** at
   **https://passwordreset.corp.example** (complete the MFA identity check).
3. Or contact the **Service Desk** — they'll verify your identity and unlock it.

## Stop it happening again
After any password change, update the password **everywhere it's saved**:
- 📱 Phone mail app (re-sign-in)
- 🗂️ Mapped network drives
- 📶 Saved Wi-Fi (corporate)
- Any app that "remembers" your work login

## Still not working?
- You keep getting locked within minutes of unlocking → you have a stale saved password somewhere;
  contact the Service Desk and mention this so they can help find the device. Reference **KB-0002**.
