# Change your Windows password

**Applies to:** staff on a company Windows PC · **KB ID:** KB-0101
**Last reviewed:** 2026-06-21 · **Owner:** Service Desk

## What this covers
How to change your Windows password while you still know your current one (e.g. it's expiring, or
you just want a new one). If you've **forgotten** it or you're **locked out**, see KB-0001 / KB-0002.

## Change it (on the company network)
1. Press **Ctrl + Alt + Delete**.
2. Click **Change a password**.
3. Type your **current** password, then your **new** password twice (rules below).
4. Press **Enter** → "Your password has been changed."

## If you're remote (on VPN)
Connect to the VPN first (KB-0003), then use **Ctrl + Alt + Delete → Change a password** as above so
the change reaches the company directory.

## Password rules
At least 12 characters with upper + lower case, a number, and a symbol; not a recent password.
A **passphrase** (three random words + a symbol) is easiest to remember.

## Important: update it everywhere
After changing it, update the saved password on your **phone mail app, mapped drives, and Wi-Fi**
or you may get **locked out** (KB-0002).

## Still not working?
Error changing it, or it won't accept a new password → contact the Service Desk; reference
**KB-0101** and include the exact message.
