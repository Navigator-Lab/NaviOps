# Wi-Fi troubleshooting

**Applies to:** staff on a company laptop · **KB ID:** KB-0004
**Last reviewed:** 2026-06-21 · **Owner:** Service Desk

## What this covers
Quick fixes when your Wi-Fi won't connect, keeps dropping, or connects but has no internet.

## Before you start
- Note **where** you are (office / home / public) and **what changed** (new location, just woke the laptop,
  after a password change).

## Try these in order
1. **Check Wi-Fi is on:** click the network icon (bottom-right). Is Wi-Fi enabled? Is **Airplane mode** off?
2. **Confirm the right network:** connect to the corporate SSID (**`CorpWiFi`**), not a guest/neighbor
   network. Avoid look-alike network names you don't recognize.
3. **Forget and reconnect:** click the network → **Forget**, then reconnect and re-enter the password.
   *(If your password recently changed, the saved Wi-Fi password may be old — KB-0001.)*
4. **Restart the adapter:** toggle Wi-Fi **off and on**; if still stuck, **restart the laptop** (clears
   most connection glitches).
5. **Connected but no internet?** Open any website. If pages don't load, restart the laptop; if you're
   remote and need internal sites, connect the **VPN** (KB-0003).
6. **Move closer / reduce interference:** weak signal? Move nearer the access point; microwaves and some
   devices interfere.

## Still not working?
- It works on your phone but not the laptop, or fails everywhere → contact the Service Desk with: your
  **location**, the **network name** you're trying, and **what happens** (no list / wrong password / no
  internet). Reference **KB-0004**.
